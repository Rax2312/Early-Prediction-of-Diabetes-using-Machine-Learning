About Diabetes

Diabetes is a chronic condition that affects how your body turns food into energy. There are three main types:

  • Type 1 Diabetes: Autoimmune condition where pancreas produces little or no insulin
  • Type 2 Diabetes: Body becomes resistant to insulin or doesn't produce enough
  • Gestational Diabetes: Occurs during pregnancy
Symptoms
  • Increased thirst
  • Frequent urination
  • Fatigue
  • Blurred vision
  • Slow-healing sores
Risk Factors
  • Family history
  • Obesity
  • Physical inactivity
  • High blood pressure
  • Poor diet
Prevention
  • Healthy eating
  • Regular exercise
  • Weight management
  • Regular checkups

Frequently Asked Questions

There is no cure for diabetes, but it can be managed with medication, lifestyle changes, and regular monitoring.

Prediabetes is a condition where blood sugar levels are higher than normal but not high enough for a diabetes diagnosis.
